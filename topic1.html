Intro

You might be wondering, "Golly gee! What could Nine Inch Nails possibly be?!?!?!?!" Well, let me tell you all about 'em.
NIN was formed by Trent Reznor in 1988, who remained the only member of the band until 2016, when Atticus Ross officially joined.
NIN belongs to the industrial rock genre, although their music sometimes leans more to the metal side.
They are very cool and awesome, as shown by the fact that they have sold over 20 million records worldwide (thank you Wikipedia!)
